About

This webiste was created by Abdul, Ruslan, Lenon and Tiernan.

Welcome to The Horror, a thrilling website created by our group as part of a class assignment. Designed with a dark, theater-inspired color scheme, this site brings the eerie world of horror movies to life. On the homepage, you will find a dynamic slider carousel showcasing covers from some of the most iconic and popular horror films. The Films page presents a collection of movie covers, making it easy to explore a variety of terrifying titles. Our News page will keep you updated with the latest news, reviews, and announcements from the world of horror cinema. The purpose of this website is to share our passion for the horror genre, offering an engaging experience for fans and newcomers alike. We hope you enjoy exploring The Horror and get a taste of the chilling entertainment that horror movies provide!
